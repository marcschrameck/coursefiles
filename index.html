Types of Snow

Throughout the winter season, there will be many different snow types falling from the sky. Temperature, pressure and freezing levels all impact water molecules held within the clouds resulting in a myriad of freezing patterns. Sometimes we get unique snowflakes, while other times we get refrozen pellets known as graupel. Here's a list of snow types:

  • plate
  • dendrite
  • column
  • needle
  • spatial dendrite
  • capped column
  • graupel
  • sleet
  • hail
Learn more at National Snow and Ice Data Center (NSDIC)
